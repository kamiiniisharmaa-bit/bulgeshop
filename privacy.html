Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026 v3.1

1. Who we are

bulgeShop (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a modern e-commerce platform dedicated to fashion and lifestyle. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

2. What we collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services. The types of data we may collect include:

  • Personal identifiers: name, email address, phone number, shipping/billing address.
  • Payment information: encrypted payment details (we do not store full credit card numbers).
  • Order history: products you purchase, returns, and preferences.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies.

We collect this data when you create an account, place an order, subscribe to our newsletter, or interact with our site.

3. How we use your data

Your personal information is used for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders, including shipping and customer support.
  • To personalize your experience and recommend products that may interest you.
  • To send order updates, promotional offers, and newsletters (you can opt out anytime).
  • To improve our website, analyze trends, and prevent fraudulent activity.
Your trust matters: We never sell or rent your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Sharing your information

We may share your data with trusted third parties only when necessary to operate our business:

  • Shipping partners: to deliver your orders (e.g., couriers, postal services).
  • Payment processors: to securely handle transactions (e.g., Stripe, PayPal).
  • Service providers: for analytics, email delivery, and customer support tools.

All third parties are contractually obligated to keep your data confidential and use it only for the services they provide to us.

5. Cookies & tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and understand how you interact with our site. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

  • Essential cookies: necessary for the website to function (e.g., shopping cart).
  • Analytics cookies: help us measure traffic and performance (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Preference cookies: remember your settings and login status.

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect some features.

6. Data security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. These include encryption (SSL), secure servers, and restricted access to personal information.

PCI compliant: Our payment processing meets the highest security standards.

7. Your rights

You have rights regarding your personal data. Depending on your location, these may include:

  • Access: request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: request that we delete your data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Opt-out: unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@bulgeShop.com.

8. Children's privacy

bulgeShop does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately so we can remove it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The latest version will always be available on this page, with the “last updated” date at the top.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of bulgeShop constitutes your acceptance of any changes.
